Bio: I am a research scientist at NVIDIA, working on computer graphics and computer vision, with a special interest in research problems related to 3D content creation. I work in the Toronto AI lab led by Prof. Sanja Fidler. Before joining NVIDIA, I received my Ph.D. degree in the year 2020 from Simon Fraser University where I worked in the GrUVi lab under the supervision of Prof. Hao (Richard) Zhang. I also worked with Prof. Hui Huang and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or who were on my supervisory committee as well. Before joining SFU in the year 2015, I worked at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I received my B.Eng. degree from Chang'an University in the year 2012.
