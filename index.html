I am a 4th year PhD student at SFU, working with Prof. Hao(Richard) Zhang, on computer graphics and related areas. In addition to Richard, my supervisory committee also includes Prof. Hui Huang and Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. Before going to SFU in the year 2015, I was a research assistant at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my Bachelor's degree from Chang'an University in the year 2012.

This summer, I am doing an internship at Adobe Research, working with Dr. Siddhartha Chaudhuri, Dr. Matthew Fisher, and Dr. Vladimir Kim.
